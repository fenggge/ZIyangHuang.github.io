Post2

Machine learning and the web have recently become pretty prevalent and popular these days in people’s lives, and its system and technology have become pretty mature and complete as well. Thus, more and more people choose to use machine learning and web to take classes because machine learning makes it much more convenient and easier for people to learn. To be more specific, machine learning always has recorded videos available on the web for people to watch. For example, Youtube collects and displays millions of videos that incorporate abundant information and lessons of almost every subject, and they are open for everyone. Therefore, people could watch the videos at any time they want and watch as many times as they want, which makes it very convenient for people. Also, when people are watching the videos, they can pause the video to take notes or go back and listen again about the part that they are confused about or they could speed up and jump to the important part as they want; thus, this characteristics of machine learning effectively improves the efficiency of studying. In addition, many of the machine learning web such as Quizlet also provides the practice questions like quizzes and tests, helping learners to better understand the concept and get ready for the official exams. Accordingly, the machine learning and web have participated into many aspects of society like education, working, and so on, and it still tries to penetrate into more fields of people’s life.
